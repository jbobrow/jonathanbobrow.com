Created the _world's smallest longest road_ to show off new features of Nanotronics' Automated Optical Inspection tool, nSpec.

It generated over [100,000 views on Reddit](https://www.reddit.com/r/boardgames/comments/1jp2q9d/worlds_smallest_settlers_of_catan_board_game/) during a typically crowded April 1st. Many of the commenters were quick to note that the game is not technically playable in its current state. So they say.
